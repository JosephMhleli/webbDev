Electrical and Mechanical system design



At Mayila Industries, we believe that there is no limit to what our imagination and ideas can achieve. That's why we offer comprehensive mechanical and electrical design services to bring your ideas to life. Our team of experts takes a systematic approach to every project, starting with research and careful analysis to determine the best course of action. We work closely with you every step of the way to ensure that your vision is fully realized.


From simulation to manufacturing, we provide complete electromechanical solutions, utilizing the latest design software such as Autodesk Inventor, CAD, and SolidWorks. Our extensive experience in the field allows us to tackle even the most complex projects with confidence, delivering high-quality results that meet your specific requirements and specifications.


  • Electromechanical Solutions: We provide electromechanical solutions, including simulations, mechanical analysis, and manufacturing.
  • Mechanical Systems Design: We offer mechanical systems design services using CAD software (SolidWorks).
  • Electrical House Wiring: We provide electrical house wiring services.
  • PCB Designs: We offer professional PCB design services.
