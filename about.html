PORTFOLIO

Mayila Industries (PTY) Ltd is a leading engineering company based in Mthatha, South Africa. Founded in October 2020 by LM Mayila, the company's mission is to provide innovative solutions to clients and fill the gap in the engineering and intelligence industries. With a headquarters located in the New Rest location of Mthatha, Mayila Industries serves clients across South Africa and beyond. Our team of highly skilled and experienced engineers is dedicated to delivering exceptional results and exceeding client expectations. We offer a range of services, including software development, automation and controls, and mechanical and electrical design. Our software development services include the design of user-friendly web and mobile applications. Our automation and controls services range from PLC programming and home automation solutions to robotics using FANUC, Kawasaki, and ABB systems. Our mechanical and electrical design services include the design of electromechanical systems, simulations and mechanical analysis, manufacturing, and electrical house wiring, as well as PCB designs. We believe in the power of technology to solve complex problems and make our world a better place, and we are committed to providing innovative solutions that minimize human effort and optimize results. Our core values of honesty, respect, leadership, intelligence, and tranquillity drive our work, and our vision is to become a leading global brand and help advance South Africa's technology sector. With a focus on quality and reliability, all our products are thoroughly tested to ensure they meet the highest standards. If you're looking for a reliable partner for engineering design, modelling, and testing, look no further than Mayila Industries.
