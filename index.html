Who we are!

MAYILA INDUSTRIES (PTY) LTD is an engineering company founded in 2020. The founder saw an unfulfilled shortage of engineering and intelligence companies in South Africa that cater for client's needs and took it upon himself to fill this gap by assembling a team of professionals that aligned with his goal and values.

Our team is skilled, trained with engineering fundamentals to solve your problems. We design small to medium sized projects and assemble projects according to customer specifications.

We value honesty, respect,leadership, intelligence and tranquility as essential driving forces to innovative excellence.

Our vision is to become a global brand and lead South Africa to a more technologically advanced country.
