Automation and Controls



The advent of the fourth industrial revolution has brought a massive transformation in the way automation is perceived and implemented. From our mobile devices to large-scale manufacturing facilities, automation has become an indispensable part of our lives, providing the means to streamline processes, reduce human effort, and achieve greater accuracy and precision.


Our team of experienced engineers offers cutting-edge automation solutions that cater to a wide range of industries, from homes and medical facilities to farms and more. Our smart automation systems are designed to simplify and automate various tasks, from basic lighting control to more complex operations like pool covers.


The automotive industry requires reliable and efficient automation systems to produce high volumes of parts with accuracy and precision. Our expertise in PLC programming and robotics enables us to provide innovative solutions that help the industry stay ahead of the curve. With experience in handling robotics systems from FANUC, Kawasaki, and ABB, we are confident in delivering world-class automation solutions for the automotive industry.


  • PLC Programming: We offer PLC programming services to automate industrial processes.
  • Home Automation Solutions: We provide home automation solutions to enhance the efficiency and convenience of homes
  • Robotics: We have experience in working with FANUC, Kawasaki, and ABB robots.
